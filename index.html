What we are looking for

The specific traits or qualities that a smoothie business would look for in an employee may vary depending on the specific type of business and its needs. However, generally speaking, some qualities that may be desirable in an employee in a smoothie business could include:

- Knowledge of nutrition and ingredients, as employees should have a good understanding of the ingredients used in smoothies and their nutritional benefits, to be able to properly advise customers.
- Customer service skills, as employees will be interacting with customers, taking orders, and answering questions.

- Ability to work quickly and efficiently, as smoothie bars often have a fast-paced environment with high volume, requiring employees to work quickly and efficiently to keep up with demand.

- Attention to detail, as smoothies often involve precise measurements and ingredient combinations to ensure consistency and quality.

- Good hygiene and food preparation skills, as employees will be handling food that is consumed raw, making it important to follow strict hygiene and food handling practices.

- Willingness to learn and adapt, as the smoothie industry is constantly evolving, with new ingredients and trends emerging, making it important for employees to stay up to date and adapt to changes.
